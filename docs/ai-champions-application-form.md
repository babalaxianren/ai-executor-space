# AI Champions Application Form
# AI 先锋申请表

## What we are looking for
## 我们在寻找什么

We are building a network of AI Champions — business operators who identify, design, and apply AI solutions within their own team. This is not an IT initiative. Champions are selected from business functions because domain expertise is what makes AI applications genuinely useful.

我们正在建立一个 AI 先锋网络——这些人是业务操盘手，能够在自己团队内识别、设计并落地 AI 解决方案。这不是一个 IT 项目。先锋来自各业务职能部门，因为领域专业知识才是让 AI 真正发挥价值的关键。

We are not looking for AI experts. We are looking for four things:

我们不是在寻找 AI 专家，而是在寻找具备以下四点特质的人：

- **Problem clarity:** You can name a specific, recurring, costly problem in your team. Not a category of problems — one concrete problem. You know the workflow in details and are confident enough to challenge how things are done today.
- **问题清晰度：** 你能指出团队中一个具体的、反复出现的、代价高昂的问题。不是一类问题，而是一个具体问题。你对工作流程了如指掌，并且有足够的信心去挑战现有的做事方式。

- **Self-starter instinct:** You have already tried AI tools on your own — without being asked to. You are curious about what they can do and honest about where they fall short. You would still be exploring AI tools even if this champion role didn't exist.
- **自驱本能：** 你已经在没有被要求的情况下自己尝试过 AI 工具。你对它们的能力充满好奇，也能坦诚面对它们的不足。即使没有这个先锋角色，你也会继续探索 AI 工具。

- **Peer credibility:** Your team respects your judgement. If you recommend something new, people give it a fair try. You have demonstrated this before.
- **同伴公信力：** 你的团队尊重你的判断。如果你推荐新事物，大家会认真尝试。你已有过这样的经历和证明。

- **Real capacity:** You are expected to find time for this. Being a Champion requires real time. You will have to do this in addition to your regular workload. There are no right or wrong answers. We are looking for specificity and honesty — not the answers you think we want to hear.
- **真实的时间投入：** 我们期望你能为此腾出时间。成为先锋需要真正的时间投入，你需要在日常工作之外承担这一角色。没有标准答案，我们寻求的是具体和诚实——而不是你认为我们想听的答案。
